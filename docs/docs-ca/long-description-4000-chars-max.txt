AndStatus is an Open Source low traffic social networking client with tree-like threaded conversations.
It supports different Social networks, including
GNU social, Mastodon, Twitter and Pump.io.
AndStatus can combine your feeds from all networks into one Timeline,
and it allows you to read and post even when you are offline.

For Android v.7.0+ devices.

Característiques diferencials de l'AndStatus:

1. Many accounts in different Social networks. Pots tenir diversos comptes de cada, escriure i respondre com qualsevol "Tu" i compartir entre comptes i sistemes.
2. You don't need to be online to read timelines and to post your updates: drafts and unsent posts are kept even after reboot. S'enviaran quan el dispositiu sigui en línia.
3. Visualització de converses en arbre.
4. "Global search" allowing you to search public messages in all networks with one query.
5. Lists of Friends and Followers, presented as User Lists and as Timelines (with the latest message of each user)
6. Els piulets, missatges, avatars i imatges adjuntes es mantenen sincronitzats en segon pla, quan el dispositiu té una bona connexió. By default, attachments are downloaded via WiFi only.
7. La memòria cau de les dades es pot emmagatzemar per anys o només per dies, tu tries.
8. Backup and restore accounts and messages between your devices. Own your data!
9. Gratuït i sense anuncis, amb el suport de la comunitat.
